About Me

I have been learning to code since January 2021, having always had an interest in how computers work. I began my coding journey by taking CS50x: Introduction to Computer Science to see if I had the capability to work in programming and to see whether it suited me. I loved it and found myself unable to stop. Better still, I found I was really good at it. Since then I have completed several certificates on FreeCodeCamp and am constantly striving to direct my learning to those areas where I can identify that I am less confident, and that will best serve to make me an exceptional coder.

I love new challenges and learning through projects and hands-on experience. To this end, in my spare time I continue to learn new skills and revise existing ones on Sololearn, to think of and create new projects in the languages I want to improve my knowledge of, and to amuse myself whilst deepening my understanding by solving problems on Codewars and Project Euler. Since March I've been a student on the Netmatters Scion Coalition Scheme where I am continuing to enhance my knowledge base with the goal of becoming a Full Stack web developer.
